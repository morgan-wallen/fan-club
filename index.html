About Morgan Wallen

Morgan Cole Wallen is an American country music singer and songwriter. Known for blending traditional country with modern pop and hip-hop influences, he has become one of the biggest stars in country music.

Born: May 13, 1993 • Sneedville, Tennessee

Genres: Country • Country Pop

Labels: Big Loud • Mercury • Republic

Years Active: 2014–Present
